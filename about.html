About

Our club is defined by a diverse group of talented and passionate individuals. Together, we make up two VEX robotics teams and one ROV robotics team. All our members bring unique skills and experiences to our club, from building and programming to designing and communicating. HKIS Robotics is a place where everyone's strengths are utilized and everyone's voice is heard. In such a strong community, our accomplishments have flourished over the years and hopefully will continue to grow in years to come.

Hong Kong Technology Challenge

Our two VEX teams attended the Hong Kong national competition for the 2018-19 season, Turning Point. Both teams won awards, including the Design and Sportsmanship Awards.

ASIJ Robotics Challenge

936A, 936B, and 936C flew to Tokyo, Japan to participate in the American School in Japan's annual VEX competition. All our teams performed well, with 936B reaching qualification rank #2!
